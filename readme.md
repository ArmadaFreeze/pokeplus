## ‚ùì FAQ

### Why is the Start button grey and not working?

You need to unlock the bot first.

- Free users: Click **"Unlock"** (top right), then select **"Free Access"**.
- Premium users: Click **"Unlock"** and log into your premium account.

---

### Why does the bot keep switching between attacks and doing nothing?

You likely did not follow every step in the installation guide.

Make sure:
- Game Resolution matches your Windows Desktop Resolution
- Bot Resolution matches the Game Resolution
- All required installation steps were followed

---

### Why does PokeMMO show 1920x1060 instead of 1920x1080?

This is a visual bug. It does not affect functionality and can be ignored.
